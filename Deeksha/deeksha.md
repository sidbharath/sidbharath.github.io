# Deeksha Learning - Organization Context

## Overview

Deeksha Vedantu is a leading educational institution specializing in integrated Pre-University (PU) college education combined with competitive exam coaching. The organization blends the best of technology and academics while nurturing children in an environment that prioritizes wellness and care.

## History

- **Founded:** 1998 by Dr. Sridhar G. and Mrs. Lalit Sridhar
- **Initial Mission:** To contribute to education in India through integrated education for competitive exam preparation
- **Major Milestone:** In 2022, Vedantu acquired a majority stake in Deeksha for $40 million, forming Deeksha Vedantu
- **Partnership Impact:** Combined Deeksha's impeccable offline pedagogical systems with Vedantu's cutting-edge online educational resources

## Educational Programs

### Core Offering
Deeksha pioneered an innovative model that bridges the gap between Board Exams and Competitive Exams by coaching students for both simultaneously under one roof.

### Programs Offered
1. **Integrated PU + Competitive Exam Coaching**
   - 2-year Board Synchronized State Board/CBSE/ICSE programs
   - JEE (Joint Entrance Examination) preparation
   - NEET (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test) preparation
   - KCET (Karnataka Common Entrance Test)
   - KVPY (Kishore Vaigyanik Protsahan Yojana)
   - CET preparation

2. **Professional Course Coaching**
   - CA (Chartered Accountancy)
   - CS (Company Secretary)

3. **Specialized Programs**
   - **Residential NEET Program:** 10-month intensive course at Vidyanagar Campus with highly qualified faculty (many holding PhDs and Master's degrees)
   - **dHybrid Learning Model:** Combines online and offline coaching methodologies

### Grade Levels Served
- Pre-University (11th and 12th grades)
- Foundation courses for competitive exam preparation

## Results and Achievements

- **96%** of students qualify for engineering exams
- **74%** qualify for NEET
- **55%** qualify for JEE Advanced
- Proven model that drives consistent outcomes

## Key Operational Challenges (from Wishlist)

The organization is looking to automate and scale operations across multiple areas:

1. **Admissions:** Lead management, content creation, sales coaching, multi-department MIS integration
2. **Operations:** Fee collection automation, teaching quality monitoring, staff behavior analysis
3. **Academic:**
   - Slip test evaluation automation
   - OMR Test ID creation (currently takes ~13 hours per week for 40 question papers)
   - Result retrieval from NTA website (challenged by captcha changes)
   - Data analysis and dashboard creation (currently takes ~2 hours per academic review)
   - JEE/NEET credential tracking (currently requires 3-4 hours daily of manual follow-up)
   - Question paper analysis and generation with controlled difficulty levels
