Executive Summary

Turo's brand shows up in ≈46% of the 13 high-intent car-rental queries we audited, but it captures only 4.4% overall share-of-voice (SoV) and averages 4.3 in ranking — good visibility when it appears, yet overshadowed by the traditional majors (Hertz, Enterprise, Avis, Sixt) that each secure double-digit SoV and blanket nearly every sub-category.

Turo dominates two niches — peer-to-peer car sharing (rank #1) and adventure/overland rentals (rank #1) — but is absent or low-ranked for mainstream, high-volume topics such as airport, family, budget and business rentals. Only one first-party citation (turo.com) was found across all answers, which limits brand authority inside ChatGPT answers that pull heavily from third-party sources such as Wikipedia, Forbes and Future Market Insights.
